Our Mission

Business Angel Portal was established with the purpose of granting emerging entrepreneurs the platform to present their projects, ideas, and startup ventures to experienced investors and accomplished businesspeople. The primary goal is straightforward: secure funding for a project, receive mentorship, or both. In a world brimming with talent, opportunities to spotlight these talents remain scarce. Business Angel Portal was conceived to address this issue and is committed to assisting startups throughout their entire lifecycle.

While the prevailing notion is that the digital realm holds the key to the future of startups, Business Angel Portal extends a warm welcome to startups hailing from every sector. From tourism and sports to culture, all industries are embraced. The mission of Business Angel Portal is to serve as a remedy by bridging the gap between untapped potential and limited opportunities.

A seasoned entrepreneur and investor, Basil Zagorodniuk established Business Angel Portal with a clear mission: to connect young entrepreneurs with experienced investors and provide the necessary support for their projects.


Understanding the challenges faced by startups, Basil Zagorodniuk envisioned Business Angel Portal as more than just a funding avenue. It's a holistic platform that offers mentorship, nurturing ideas from inception to fruition and empowering the next generation of innovators.
